A [toki pona] interpretation of "The Changes of Zhou": the 卦辭 hexagram initial commentary from the Chinese classic 易經 (Yi Jing / I Ching).
Started by jan Lesate (Alexander) in 2023…


### Acknowledgements

Much is based on Part II of [John Minford]'s English interpretation, including the names of the hexagrams (written in [sitelen sitelen]).

The [sitelen pona] font is [linja pona](https://musilili.net/linja-pona/) by jan Same<!-- [Lipamanka](https://lipamanka.gay)'s [Linja Lipamanka](https://lipamanka.gay/linjamanka) -->.

### Notes & common terms

八卦
: linja mute — trigram, [bagua](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bagua)

元
: ...??

亨
: alasa sona — divination, "augury" [Minford]; 'searching for knowledge'

利
: tenpo kama — fortune; 'future'

贞
: wawa — auspicious; 'good'

大人
: jan wawa — 'great person'

君子
: jan pona — 'good person'

小人
: jan pona ala — 'not-good person'

涉大川
: pali suli — literally 'crossing a great river', but commonly understood to be a metaphor for commenting an important undertaking or "hazardous enterprise" [Minford]

孚
: ilo mani — Minford interprets as spoils of war, "captives" [Minford]; 'valuable things'

# 01

䷀ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Kan</span> <!-- kân -->
: sinpin la: sewi
:  monsi la: sewi

元亨, 利贞
: alasa sona li pona
: tenpo kama li wawa


# 02

䷁ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Kewen</span> <!-- k'wen -->

: sinpin la: ma
:  monsi la: ma

元亨, 利牝马之贞. 君子有攸往, 先迷后得主, 利西南得朋, 东北丧朋. 安贞, 吉
: alasa sona li pona
: tenpo kama la: soweli li ken mama
: jan sewi li kama tawa ma ante la: tenpo kama li nasa
: jan sewi ni la: tenpo pini li sewi
: ma weka wan li jo e ilo pona
: ma weka ante li weka e ilo pona
: tenpo kama li pakala ala li wawa


# 03

䷂ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Tewen</span> <!-- d'wen -->

: alasa sona li pona
: tenpo kama li wawa
: kama ala tawa ma ante
: wawa la: jan sewi li jo e jan pona

䷃ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Mun</span> <!-- mung -->
: sinpin la: nena
:  monsi la: telo-tawa

亨. 匪我求童蒙, 童蒙求我. 初筮告, 再三渎, 渎则不告. 利贞.
: _o alasa sona_
: mi alasa ala e kasi la: kasi li alasa e mi
: wan la: sona alasa li pona
: tu mute la: sona alasa li jaki
: tenpo kama li wawa
