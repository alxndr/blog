A [Toki Pona] interpretation of each hexagram's 卦辭 (initial commentary) from the Chinese classic 易經 (Yi Jing / I Ching), started by jan Lesate (Alexander) in 2023…

"Crossing a great stream" is often thought to be a metaphor for an important undertaking. Therefore I have interpreted it as "pali suli" rather than referring to traveling or water.

(Note that terms in italics are pending further consideration.)


### Acknowledgements

Much is based on Part II of [John Minford]'s English interpretation, including the names of the hexagrams (written in Sitelen Sitelen).

Sitelen Pona font is [linja pona](https://musilili.net/linja-pona/) by jan Same<!-- [Lipamanka](https://lipamanka.gay)'s [Linja Lipamanka](https://lipamanka.gay/linjamanka) -->.

# 01

䷀ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Kan</span>
: sinpin la: sewi
:  monsi la: sewi

元亨, 利贞
: alasa sona li pona
: tenpo kama li pona


# 02

䷁ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Kewen</span>
: sinpin la: ma
:  monsi la: ma

元亨, 利牝马之贞. 君子有攸往, 先迷后得主, 利西南得朋, 东北丧朋. 安贞, 吉
: alasa sona li pona
: tenpo kama la: soweli li ken mama
: jan sewi li kama tawa ma ante la: tenpo kama li nasa
: jan sewi ni la: tenpo pini li sewi
: ma weka wan li jo pona
: ma weka ante li weka pona
: tenpo kama li pakala ala li pona


# 03

䷂ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Tewen</span>
: sinpin la: telo tawa
:  monsi la: kalama

元亨, 利贞, 勿用有攸往, 利建侯.
: alasa sona li pona
: tenpo kama li pona
: kama ala tawa ma ante
: pona la: jan sewi li jo e jan pona


# 04

䷃ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Mun</span>
: sinpin la: nena
:  monsi la: telo tawa

亨. 匪我求童蒙, 童蒙求我. 初筮告, 再三渎, 渎则不告. 利贞.
: _o alasa sona_
: mi alasa ala e kasi la: kasi li alasa e mi
: sona alasa wan li pona la: sona alasa mute li jaki
: tenpo kama li pona


# 05

䷄ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Senju</span>
: sinpin la: telo tawa
:  monsi la: sewi

有孚, 光亨, 贞吉. 利涉大川.
: _jan pi poki awen_ <!-- "captives"… or "having trust" ? --> la: tenpo kama li suno
: pali suli la: tenpo kama li pona


# 06

䷅ <span class="toki" data-sitelen="true" data-sitelen-ratio="2">Sijun</span>
: sinpin la: sewi
:  monsi la: telo tawa

有孚, 窒. 惕中吉. 终凶. 利见大人, 不利涉大川.
: _jan pi poki awen_ <!-- "captives"… or "having trust" ? --> la: pake <!-- TODO glyph not in linja-lipamanka -->
: _fear/respect middle/center_ li pona
: pini li jaki
: weka e jan suli la: pona
: pali suli la: tenpo kama li pona ala
