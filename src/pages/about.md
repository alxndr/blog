### other sites

I am Tech Lead (volunteer) for [KGLW.net](https:kglw.net), a fan-run site about the band **King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard**. It uses a SaaS called Songfish to power the main site and API; I spun up [KGLW.today](https://kglw.today) using the API to highlight interesting anniversaries in the band's history.

I created the site [Almost-Dead.net](https://almost-dead.net) to track the performances of the Grateful Dead "cover" band **Joe Russo's Almost Dead**.
