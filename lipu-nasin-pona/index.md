# Notes

* 道 — Tao / Dao; "The Way"[^Muller] — nasin
* 德 — Dé; goodness, virtuous; "Virtue"[^Muller] — pona
* 圣人 — "The Sage"[^Muller] — jan sewi
* 玄 — mysterious, unknowable — nasa
* 冬渉川 — "crossing an ice-covered river"[^Muller] — tenpo ike la pali suli
